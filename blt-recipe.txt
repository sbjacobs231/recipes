Ingredients
- 1/2 lb bacon
- 8 slices white bread
- 8 leaves iceberg lettuce, fresh and full
- 8 slices of ripened tomatoes
- 8 tablespoon's Hellmann's mayonnaise

Directions
1. Cook bacon until cripsy, then drain on paper towels.
2. Toast the 8 slices of bread.
3. Spread 1 tablespoon mayo on each slice of toasted bread.
4. Add 1 slice of lettuce to 4 pieces of may-spreaded toast.
5. Add 2 slices of tomato on top of lettuce.
6. Arrange 3 slices of bacon evenly on top of tomato.
7. Add 1 slice of lettuce on top of bacon.
8. Put the remaining 4 pieces of mayo-spreaded toast on top to finish the sandwiches.
9. Enjoy!
